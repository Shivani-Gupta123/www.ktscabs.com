Gorakhpur, the city of nawabs, is a treasure trove of history, culture, and culinary delights. Nestled in the heart of Uttar Pradesh, this city offers a diverse range of experiences for travelers.

We will take you on a journey through the best places to visit in and around Gorakhpur, with Safar Cabs as your trusted travel partner.

1. Bara Imambara: An Architectural Marvel

Located in the heart of Gorakhpur, the Bara Imambara is an architectural masterpiece. Built by Nawab Asaf-ud-Daula in the 18th century, it is a prime example of Mughal architecture. 

2. Rumi Darwaza: The Turkish Gateway

Known as the 'Gateway to Gorakhpur,' the Rumi Darwaza is a symbol of the city. Its intricate design and grandeur make it a must-visit landmark. 

3. Chota Imambara: The Palace of Lights

Just a stone's throw away from Bara Imambara, the Chota Imambara is another architectural gem. 

4. Hazratganj: Shopper's Paradise

Hazratganj is Gorakhpur's commercial hub, offering everything from traditional chikan embroidery to modern fashion. It's the perfect place to shop for souvenirs and immerse yourself in the local shopping culture.

5. A Walk in Gorakhpur's Parks

Enjoy a leisurely walk in Gorakhpur's lush green parks like Janeshwar Mishra Park and Ambedkar Memorial Park.
