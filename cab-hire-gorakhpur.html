Things to Know About Gorakhpur

Gorakhpur is a historical city situated in the Purvanchal region of the state of Uttar Pradesh. It also finds its mention in much historical documentation for instance, the Ain-i-Akbari describes it as the capital of the Awadh suba. The most widely known attribute of Gorakhpur is the Geeta Press, a globally known publishing house trusted for its copies of several Hindu scriptures and legends. Gorakhpur derives its name form Gorakshpuram which means the dwelling of Shree Gorakhnath who is the presiding deity here with a magnificent temple devoted to him.
